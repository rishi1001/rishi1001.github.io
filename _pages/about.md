# Rishi Shah
I am an incoming MSML student @ CMU. My interests are generative-AI, LLM and multimodal AI. Currently, I am working in Samsung Headquaters in South Korea. I work in the Generative AI team where my current project is to develop a fine-tuned LORA models over  Samsung' Stable Diffusion model to generate images of various themes based on user preferences. Previously, I worked on RAG based application to answer user's query in a conversational way from a given e-manual pdf. During my time as an undergraduate in CS at IIT-Delhi, I worked with Prof. Sayan Ranu and Prof. Sourav Medya on my B.Tech thesis on developing NEUROCUT, a novel GNN based approach to extract cut from any graph that satisfy a given user objective. I have published works on multi-agent RL and price forecasting in my time at IIT-Delhi. I have developed various course projects related to computer vision, NLP, RL and GNN and have aced in those courses as well.
